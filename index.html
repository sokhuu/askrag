What does Rule 3110 require for branch office inspections?
Rule 3110(c) requires firms to inspect each branch according to a schedule based on its risk designation — at minimum, every three years for regular branches, annually for OSJs and supervisory branches meeting certain criteria.
Source: FINRA Rule 3110(c) — Internal Inspections
Does that apply to a one-person branch too?
Yes, but firms have some flexibility for smaller offices — inspections can be unannounced or conducted remotely if the firm's risk assessment supports it. I can walk through what a written inspection schedule needs to cover, if that'd help.
Source: FINRA Rule 3110.12 — Guidance on Branch Inspections
