I'm opening a home bakery in Long Beach. Do I need a permit?
Yes — home-based food businesses in California fall under the Homemade Food Act (AB 1616 / the updated Cal. Homemade Food Operations Act). You'll need a permit from LA County Public Health and a city home-occupation permit from Long Beach.
Source: CA Homemade Food Operations Act · LA County EH-101
What's that going to cost me total?
Roughly $170–$260 for the county permit plus a $30–$90 city home-occupation fee, depending on your class tier. I've added both to your Exit 3 checklist with the current fee schedule.
Source: LA County EH fee schedule, 2026
